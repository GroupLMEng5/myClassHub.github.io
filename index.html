The aim of this webpage is to allow students to access information about classes they will be taking in the future. This information is provided by current students, and will be in a less formal format - allowing for tips and advice to be passed between year groups in the department.
The classes are divided by year group. As well as this, they are colour coded to indicate which degree stream they occur in.
Compulsory classes for all streams are coloured Blue
Elective classes are coloured Orange
Classes specific to Mechanical Engineering are coloured Red
Classes specific to Aeronautical Engineering are coloured Green
