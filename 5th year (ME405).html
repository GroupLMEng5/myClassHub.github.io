ME405 - Heat and Flow 4

×

Summary

Heat and Flow 4 developed on heat transfer and fluid dynamics from previous years. The content is heavily focussed on conduction, convection, radiation and mass transfer. The class ran for one semester and was graded via a myplace quiz (20%) sat under exam conditions and a final exam (80%) in the december exam diet. Heat and Flow 4 was a 10-credit class and was densely calculation-based.

Exam (80%)

Online Quiz (20%)



Overall, the class was seen as difficult but at the standard expected for a level 4 class. The lectures and tutorials were of a high standard and assisted in achieving full comprehension of the course which was essential to achieving a high mark. The knowledge gained from previous Heat and Flow classes was fundamental to achieving this understanding.
