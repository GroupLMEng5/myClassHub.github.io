ME107 - Experimental and Laboratory Skills



×

Summary

This class was an introduction to the practices required when working within a laboratory or carrying out an experiment. Students were also taught; elements of laboratory and workshop safety, risk assessment procedures and the correct structure, format and writing style of a technical report. The class was worth 10 credits and assessment consisted of a group report and participation in discussion on an online forum.

The class consisted of mainly practical work and most students found it more enjoyable than challenging. The class assessment strategy was deemed fair and manageable and the objectives of these were closely related to the content of the class. The material was not closely related to many other first year classes, however the skills acquired will be invaluable throughout the rest of the course.

Difficulty: 4.8
Overall Satisfaction: 7.3
(10 = Very Difficult, 1 = Very Easy)

Most students found this class to be more enjoyable than challenging.
