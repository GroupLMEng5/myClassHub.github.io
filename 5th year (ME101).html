ME101 - Heat and Flow 1

×

Summary

Summary of contents of ME101



Difficulty: 6.9
Overall Satisfaction: 7

(10 = Very Difficult, 1 = Very Easy)

- "Personally found it quite difficult, as the concept is quite different from the usual stuff learnt from high school. Though with enough time and effort (which is quite a lot), it is generally manageable."

- "I didn't think the class was extremely hard but it was at a challenging enough level for first year of uni. The lectures were easy to follow and you had opportunities to enquire about anything you didn't follow. The tutorials were extremely useful in prep for exam."

- "Challenging in the sense that it was new material but made sense after doing the tutorials - by the end of the year was my favourite class!"

Overall students found that a lot of the material was challenging due to the fact it was unlike anything studied at school. However generally students found that they were rewarded for the work they put in to the class. The tutorials and past papers proved highly useful for developing a greater understanding of the topic.

- "I felt as though there were a lot of compulsory quizzes/tutorials, however these proved very helpful when it came to the exam. Although the quizzes themselves never took up too much time, grasping the topics beforehand, I found, was very time consuming as there was a lot of material to cover. Most of the exam papers related to what we were learning in class, however more examples closer to the actual exam questions themselves would have been very helpful during revision. Most of the time with the class tests, you would simply get a mark at the end, however we were able to discuss our papers with the lecturer afterwords if we wanted to know exactly where we went wrong in each question."

- "Assessments were fair"

- "There were only 4 online quizzes throughout the full year, which were fairly straight forward. The time given was pretty reasonable as we had almost a full day to complete each one."

The assessment of the class was deemed fair by the majority of students. The online quizzes provided a way of breaking up the marks for the class and assisted the learning process throughout the year. However the style of questions was entirely different to the exam.

- "Did not help with other classes in first year but links with others in second year Since ME101 I have used the knowledge learnt to help me in my ME203 class, my second year mathematical modelling class and my flight and space flight elective. When studying this class I did have to use skills learnt from my maths class."

- "Pretty well, some Examples turned out to be real engineering problems"

- "As Heat and Flow was mostly an entirely new subject for me, it was quite difficult to get my head around at first. I found it was quite different from my other classes as most of them were either focused on electrics, MATLAB or mechanics. Continuing onto this year however, I feel they merged the two classes very well in that they introduced the jump between first year and second year Heat and Flow gradually at first."

Students found this class didn't relate to other first year classes. However, the knowledge gained from the class this year became essential for further Heat and Flow classes and highly relevant to many other topics studied in later years of the course.

- "The material was good, past papers, lecture slides and tutorials were useful and consistent. The revision lecture was useful as they went over stuff we asked them. Didn't like the quizzes on myplace as they were just a numerical answer - no working."

- "The lecture slides are quite helpful, and usually illustrated fairly well; tutorials are quite well organised for each topic of learning as well. Plenty past exam papers were given."

- "Good selection of example questions and past papers were provided. However, it was frustrating that solutions to tutorials were only provided closer to the exam periods."

Students found that the material for the class was sufficient with consistently informative lecture slides and thorough tutorials. The lack of exam solutions did prove challenging for some students however.
